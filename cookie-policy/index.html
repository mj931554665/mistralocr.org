Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that are used to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Provide personalized experiences

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function:

  • Maintaining your login status
  • Remembering language preferences
  • Ensuring secure website operation

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our website:

  • Page visit statistics
  • User behavior analysis
  • Performance monitoring

Functional Cookies

Provide enhanced user experience:

  • Saving user preferences
  • Remembering last used settings

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use cookies from the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics - for website analytics
  • Google Tag Manager - for tracking code management

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through:

  • Changing cookie permissions in browser settings
  • Deleting existing cookies
  • Choosing to reject certain types of cookies

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality

5. Cookie Duration

Our cookies have the following durations:

  • Session Cookies - expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies - remain for up to 12 months

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. For significant changes, we will:

  • Post notifications on the website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Request your consent when necessary
